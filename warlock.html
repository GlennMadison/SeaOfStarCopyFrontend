Warlock
A young orphan discovered an ancient grimoire filled with forbidden knowledge. Delving deep into the dark arts, he became obsessed with power and transformed into the formidable Warlock. Clad in midnight-black robes with glowing eyes, he wields arcane spells and curses, seeking to unravel the mysteries of Eldoria while bending reality to his will. Feared and respected, the Warlock roams the lands, using his dark powers to protect or dominate the world.
Warlocks trace their origins to a forgotten age, born from a celestial cataclysm. Marked by the ethereal force known as the Shadow Veil, one survivor, isolated and shunned, sought solace within forbidden tomes. Embracing their identity as the Warlock, they don tattered robes adorned with runes, wielding a crackling staff. With command over shadowy minions, devastating spells, and bound spirits, the Warlock's tortured past fuels an insatiable thirst for power. Manipulating the balance between light and darkness, they alter destiny, forever seeking ultimate supremacy.

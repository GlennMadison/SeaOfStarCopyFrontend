Heroes

Warlock
Warlocks derive their magic from forming pacts with supernatural beings. These pacts grant them unique spells and abilities, making them formidable and versatile spellcasters.
Knight
The Knight hails from a noble lineage, raised with a strict code of chivalry and honor. They have been trained in the art of combat and are well-versed in the ways of courtly etiquette.
Adventurer
Adventurers have an insatiable curiosity and a thirst for exploration. They are eager to uncover hidden treasures, discover ancient ruins, and chart unexplored territories.
Rogue
Rogues possess remarkable agility and acrobatic prowess. They can scale walls, perform daring flips, and evade traps and obstacles with ease, making them exceptional in both urban and wilderness environments.

Enemies

Doomgnarls
Doomgnarls possess a natural affinity for arcane magic. They excel in spellcasting, particularly in the realms of illusion, enchantment, and darkness-based spells.
Scorchers
Scorchers are mischievous creatures born from the elemental plane of fire. They delight in causing chaos, setting things ablaze, and playing tricks on unsuspecting individuals.
Wispereons
Wispereons are the spirits of the deceased, bound to the mortal realm. They exist in a semi-translucent state, able to pass through solid objects and float above the ground.
Blightglis
Blightglis are smaller in stature compared to other humanoid races. Their small size and nimble frames allow them to maneuver through tight spaces and go unnoticed.
